Your Dream Trip to New York

Explore the city that never sleeps, from its iconic landmarks to its vibrant neighborhoods, and experience the energy of New York City.

Statue of Liberty

Begin your New York adventure with a visit to the iconic Statue of Liberty. Learn about the history of this symbol of freedom and enjoy the stunning views of the Manhattan skyline from Liberty Island.

Times Square

Step into the heart of Manhattan and experience the bright lights and bustling energy of Times Square, a must-see destination that truly captures the spirit of New York City.

Central Park

Take a break from the urban hustle and unwind in the natural beauty of Central Park. Whether you stroll through its scenic paths or visit landmarks like Bethesda Terrace, this green oasis offers a peaceful retreat in the middle of the city.

3-Day New York Itinerary

Day 1: Statue of Liberty & Ellis Island

Kick off your trip with a ferry ride to the Statue of Liberty, followed by a visit to Ellis Island, where you can explore the Immigration Museum. End the day with a dinner in Lower Manhattan.

Day 2: Times Square & Broadway

Spend the day in Midtown, starting with a visit to Times Square. Explore nearby attractions like Rockefeller Center, and in the evening, catch a Broadway show.

Day 3: Central Park & Museum Mile

Enjoy a peaceful morning in Central Park, followed by a visit to Museum Mile, home to famous museums like The Met and the Guggenheim. Conclude your trip with a walk down Fifth Avenue.
