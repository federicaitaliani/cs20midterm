Your Dream Trip to Paris

Explore the City of Lights with a journey filled with iconic landmarks, rich culture, and unforgettable memories.

Eiffel Tower

Your trip starts with a visit to the most iconic monument in the world, the Eiffel Tower. Take in stunning panoramic views of Paris from the top, and enjoy a romantic dinner at its elegant restaurant.

Louvre Museum

Immerse yourself in art and history at the Louvre, home to masterpieces such as the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo. Stroll through the museum's grand halls and experience its vast collection of artworks from all over the world.

Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur

Wander through the charming streets of Montmartre, the artist's district of Paris. Climb up to the Sacré-Cœur Basilica for a breathtaking view of the city, and visit local cafés where famous artists once found inspiration.

3-Day Paris Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Eiffel Tower

Arrive in Paris and settle into your hotel. In the evening, take a sunset tour of the Eiffel Tower, followed by a Seine River cruise.

Day 2: Louvre & Notre-Dame

Spend the day exploring the Louvre, followed by a visit to the historic Notre-Dame Cathedral. Enjoy dinner at a classic Parisian bistro.

Day 3: Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur

On your final day, stroll through the artistic neighborhood of Montmartre, and visit the Sacré-Cœur Basilica for one last panoramic view of Paris.
